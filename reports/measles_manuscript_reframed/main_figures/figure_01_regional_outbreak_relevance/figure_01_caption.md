Figure 1. Regional outbreak relevance and immunity gap motivating antiviral preparedness.

Panel A summarizes the source-verified escalation of measles in Mexico across official federal and PAHO/WHO reporting. Panel B shows national MMR1 and MMR2 coverage for Mexico in 2025 relative to the 95% two-dose threshold used to prevent sustained outbreaks. In the reframed manuscript, this figure establishes why antiviral preparedness is regionally relevant, but it does not define the therapeutic target space.
