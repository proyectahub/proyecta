The compound-selection framework was tiered by intent. Clinically interpretable repurposing candidates populated the translational screening layer, whereas ERDRP-0519, AS-48, and FIP were retained as structural comparators to validate pocket recognition and mechanistic plausibility rather than to inflate the repurposing shortlist. Accordingly, the L campaign was organized around translational small-molecule screening with ERDRP-0519 as a direct polymerase benchmark, while the F campaign was intentionally more conservative and used AS-48 and FIP to define and validate the prefusion entry pocket before broader exploratory expansion.
