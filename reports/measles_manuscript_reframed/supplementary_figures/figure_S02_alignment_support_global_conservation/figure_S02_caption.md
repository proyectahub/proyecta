Supplementary Figure S2. Multiple-sequence-alignment support for global conservation of measles virus F and L druggable pockets.

Panels A and C show full-length consensus-frequency profiles derived from MAFFT alignments of the curated F and L amino-acid panels, respectively. Vertical gold markers denote docking-relevant pocket residues mapped from the 5YZC prefusion F inhibitor complex and the 9OCF ERDRP-0519 polymerase complex. Panels B and D show condensed alignment excerpts restricted to pocket positions, with rows sorted by region and deviation count and residues colored by physicochemical class. Non-consensus residues are labeled directly with their amino-acid letter. This figure provides direct alignment-level support for the conservation claims summarized in the main-text pocket-conservation figure.
