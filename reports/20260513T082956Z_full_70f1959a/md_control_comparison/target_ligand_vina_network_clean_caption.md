**Figure. Target-ligand docking relationship map for MD follow-up.** The diagram summarizes the docking-score relationships between the two Andes orthohantavirus structural targets and the ligands selected for MD contextualization. Blue edges correspond to `5E04` and burgundy edges to `9P3Y`; each edge label reports the best AutoDock Vina score in kcal/mol for the corresponding receptor-ligand pair. Edge thickness scales with the absolute score magnitude, so thicker edges indicate more favorable Vina scores within this comparison set. Green nodes are repurposing candidates selected from the approved-source screen, orange nodes are antiviral computational reference controls, and purple denotes the 9P3Y crystallographic NAG redocking control. The map is intended to visualize score context and target-ligand relationships before MD; it does not imply experimental binding or antiviral efficacy.
