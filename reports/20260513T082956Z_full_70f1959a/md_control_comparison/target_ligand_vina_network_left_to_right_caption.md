**Figure. Left-to-right target-ligand docking map for MD follow-up.** Ligands and controls are shown on the left and viral targets on the right to emphasize that the selected candidates and controls were connected computationally to the two docking targets. Blue edges correspond to `5E04`; burgundy edges correspond to `9P3Y`. Score labels are arranged in target-specific columns to avoid overlap and report AutoDock Vina scores in kcal/mol. More negative values are more favorable under the Vina scoring function, and edge width scales with the absolute score magnitude for visualization only. Green nodes are repurposing candidates, orange nodes are antiviral computational references, and the purple node is the 9P3Y NAG structural/redocking control. The diagram contextualizes the systems chosen for MD and should not be interpreted as evidence of experimental binding or antiviral activity.
