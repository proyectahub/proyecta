Figure 1. Official escalation of measles in Mexico and the 2025 immunization gap. (A) Cumulative confirmed measles cases reported for Mexico in official Ministry of Health documents, WHO/PAHO outbreak reporting, and a PAHO epidemiological alert. The y-axis is shown on a logarithmic scale because source-verified case counts increased from 22 to 10,920 between March 2025 and May 2026. Point labels indicate the reported territorial spread and deaths when available. (B) National MMR coverage reported by PAHO for Mexico in 2025. The dashed line marks the 95% two-dose coverage threshold recommended by WHO/PAHO to prevent outbreaks. Source release dates were 12 March 2025, 4 April 2025, 28 April 2025, 23 May 2025, and 29 May 2026; source-specific epidemic cutoffs are listed in the source table.
