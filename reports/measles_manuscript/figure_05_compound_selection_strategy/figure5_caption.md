Figure 5. Strategy for selecting compounds for L-P and F docking campaigns. (A) Tiered selection logic separating public antiviral chemistry from MeV target-specific controls. Compounds were included only if they had a public chemistry identifier or a target-engaged MeV structural anchor, and then assigned either to the primary L-P repurposing arm or to the secondary F validation arm. (B) Screen-ready assignment table showing that remdesivir, favipiravir, and ribavirin form the clinically oriented L-P repurposing set; ERDRP-0519 is retained as the direct polymerase benchmark; and AS-48 together with FIP define the prefusion F control set. Controls were retained for pocket validation and pose recovery rather than being treated as repurposed drug leads.
