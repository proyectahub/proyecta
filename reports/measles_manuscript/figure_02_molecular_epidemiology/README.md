# Figure 2 package

Contents:
- `figure2_molecular_epidemiology.png` and `.svg`: manuscript-ready figure exports.
- `figure2_official_genotype_timeline.csv`: official genotype events extracted from the federal bulletin.
- `figure2_nuccore_audit_all_records.csv`: all nuccore records returned by the broad query, with audit flags.
- `figure2_strict_mexico_n_gene_records.csv`: strict Mexican N-gene subset used in panel C.
- `figure2_molecular_summary.csv`: panel B and D summary metrics.
- `figure2_source_excerpts.md`: short evidence excerpts and query notes.
- `figure2_caption.md`: manuscript legend draft.
- `figure2_results.md`: matching Results paragraph draft.
- `raw_sources/`: downloaded XML, JSON, and PDF sources.
- `source_text/`: normalized extracted text for the official bulletin and article abstract.
