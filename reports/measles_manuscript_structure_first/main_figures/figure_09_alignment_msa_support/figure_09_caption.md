Figure 9. Sequence-logo and conservation-track support for the conservation of measles virus F and L druggable pockets.

Panels A and C show compact PDB cartoon projections of the prefusion-F and ERDRP-0519 pockets, respectively, with conserved core residues highlighted in blue and the surrounding contact shell shown in pale grey. Panels B and D summarize full-length MAFFT-derived conservation profiles for the curated F and L amino-acid panels, respectively, with the crystallographically defined pocket residues marked in gold. Panels E and F present pocket-restricted sequence logos that compress the underlying multiple-sequence alignments into residue-level information content, making the dominant consensus residues and the limited variability at the pocket positions visually explicit. The logo-based layout is more compact and more conventional for a main-text figure than a full alignment matrix, while the complete pocket alignments are retained in the supplementary alignment figure.
