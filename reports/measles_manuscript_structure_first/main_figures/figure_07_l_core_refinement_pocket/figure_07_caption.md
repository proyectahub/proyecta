Figure 7. Conservation-validated refinement of the ERDRP-0519 binding pocket in the measles virus L polymerase.

Panel A shows a principal-component projection of the full 9OCF chain-A C-alpha trace with the ERDRP-0519 contact shell overlaid. Grey circles mark all 31 residues within 6 A of the co-bound ligand, blue circles identify the 15-residue conserved core retained for refinement (contact distance <=4 A and consensus frequency = 1.0 across the curated L alignment), and orange diamonds mark the subset of ERDRP-0519 atoms used to derive the refined docking box. Panel B shows the contact-shell conservation profile, illustrating that the conserved core is fully invariant while the only contact-shell residues below complete invariance are K535, R544, and I739. Panel C compares the initial first-pass docking box derived from the full ERDRP-0519 pose with the stricter refinement box derived from the conserved core. The refinement box is centered at (172.236, 168.128, 188.918) with dimensions 17.033 x 12.000 x 17.607 A.
